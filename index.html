WHO IS THE GREATEST HEALER?

Undoubtedly, the body is the greatest healer. No medicine or treatment can heal the body. If given time and space, our body knows how to heal and cure itself. Medicines and procedures only assist in creating the right environment for the body to repair itself.

  • - The impact of toxins become stronger over a period of time and manifests as a disease. If there is a genetic.
  • - Predisposition then the impact of the toxin build-up becomes even more severe.

  • - Immunity power of the body. Finally Ayurveda insists on a wholesome lifestyle which will prevent a recurrence of the disease.
